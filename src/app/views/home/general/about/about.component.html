What is Tybo Fashion?

Tybo Fashion is an online marketplace with a focus on Mzansi fashion products. The platform was founded by Tybo Solution (South African Sofware company) as they wanted to help people discover, buy and sell fashion. It features fashion designers and resellers - you'll find gems that range in price depending on your budget!. We accept designer/resellers who are committed to deliver quality work.

On Tybo Fashion, it's easy to not only discover, but also connect with emerging brands and the creators behind them. Because each designer on Tybo Fashion is in charge of managing their own shop, fulfilling orders, and setting their brand’s policies, any questions regarding shipping details, delivery times, sizing, order specifics, and returns should be directed to the brand owner of the shop.

You can find each brand owner’s contact information on their site profile page.

Feel free to join our Facebook and Instagram pages, links are in the footer.

Tybo Fashion allows shoppers to discover, follow, and engage with independent designer/maker brands with unique products all in South Africa.
